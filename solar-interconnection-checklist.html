Solar Interconnection Checklist

Use this checklist to prepare a clean submittal. We’ll finalize and stamp as needed.

  1. One-line diagram including service, inverter(s), PV output, OCPD, grounding/bonding
  2. Load calculations (per NEC 220, 705)
  3. Inverter & module spec sheets
  4. Rapid shutdown documentation (if applicable)
  5. Placards & labels per NEC 690
  6. Utility/AHJ interconnection form(s)
  7. Site plan & equipment layout
